‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?
We typically respond within 24 hours for standard issues. Critical issues are prioritized and may receive a response within 2-4 hours during business hours.
What information should I include in a bug report?
Please include: 1) Steps to reproduce the issue, 2) What you expected to happen, 3) What actually happened, 4) Your browser and device information, 5) Screenshots if possible.
Can I request new features?
Absolutely! We welcome feature requests. Please use the "Feature Request" option in the report form and describe how the feature would improve your experience.
Is my data safe when I report issues?
Yes, we take privacy seriously. Your reports are stored securely and we only use the information to improve our services. Email addresses are optional and used only for follow-ups.
What if I have a security concern?
For security concerns, please select "Security Concern" as the issue type and provide detailed information. We treat security issues with the highest priority and confidentiality.
